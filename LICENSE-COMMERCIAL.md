## 📜 Overview

Omnilearn is open source under the **AGPL v3 license** — free forever for individuals, researchers, non-profits, and open-source projects.

However, if you want to use Omnilearn in a **closed-source product**, **proprietary SaaS**, or **commercial offering** without open-sourcing your modifications, you need a **commercial license**.

## 📋 Licensing FAQ

### Q: Can I use Omnilearn internally without a commercial license?

**A:** Yes! AGPL v3 allows internal use without restrictions. You only need a commercial license if you:
- Offer Omnilearn as a SaaS to external users
- Embed it in a proprietary product
- Don't want to open-source your modifications

### Q: What happens if I don't buy a commercial license?

**A:** You can still use Omnilearn under AGPL v3, but you must:
- Open-source any modifications you make
- Make your source code available to users
- Include AGPL v3 license in your distribution
- Provide source code to anyone who receives your software

### Q: Can I upgrade from Startup to Enterprise later?

**A:** Yes! You can upgrade anytime. The upgrade fee is the difference between licenses, prorated for time elapsed.

### Q: Do you offer discounts for startups/non-profits?

**A:** Yes! We offer:
- **50% discount** for pre-seed/seed startups (raise <$2M)
- **50% discount** for registered non-profits
- **Free license** for open-source projects (with approval)
- **Free license** for academic research (with publication rights)

### Q: What payment methods do you accept?

**A:** 
- Wire transfer (USD, EUR, GBP)
- Credit card (via Stripe)
- Crypto (USDC, USDT, BTC, ETH)
- Invoice (for Enterprise+ licenses, Net-30 terms)

### Q: Is there a trial period?

**A:** Yes! We offer a **30-day pilot program** for $500 (credited toward license purchase if you proceed).

### Q: Can I customize the license terms?

**A:** Yes! For Enterprise and OEM licenses, we can negotiate custom terms based on your needs. Contact us to discuss.
