Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Omnilearn internally without a commercial license?

Yes! AGPL v3 allows internal use without restrictions. You only need a commercial license if you offer Omnilearn as a SaaS to external users, embed it in a proprietary product, or don't want to open-source your modifications.

What happens if I don't buy a commercial license?

You can still use Omnilearn under AGPL v3, but you must open-source any modifications you make and make your source code available to users.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes! We offer 50% discounts for pre-seed/seed startups (raise <$2M), registered non-profits, and free licenses for open-source projects and academic research.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes! You can upgrade from Startup to Enterprise anytime. The upgrade fee is the difference between licenses, prorated for time elapsed.

What payment methods do you accept?

Wire transfer (USD, EUR, GBP), credit card (Stripe), crypto (USDC, USDT, BTC, ETH), and invoice for Enterprise+ licenses (Net-30 terms).
