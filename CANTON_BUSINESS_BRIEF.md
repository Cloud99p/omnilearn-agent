## 🚀 Why Canton?

### 1. **Privacy-Preserving**
- **Problem**: Banks can't share data on public blockchains
- **Canton Solution**: Privacy domains keep sensitive data isolated
- **Benefit**: Regulatory compliance (GDPR, banking secrecy)

### 2. **Interoperable**
- **Problem**: Banks use different ledgers (Hyperledger, Corda, custom)
- **Canton Solution**: Connects independent ledgers into unified ecosystem
- **Benefit**: Cross-bank settlements without central counterparty

### 3. **Compliant by Design**
- **Problem**: Public blockchains can't enforce regulations
- **Canton Solution**: DAML contracts enforce KYC/AML/jurisdiction rules
- **Benefit**: Automatic compliance at the protocol level

### 4. **Proven Scale**
- **Fact**: **$6T+** in real-world assets already tokenized on Canton
- **Users**: JPMorgan, UBS, Deutsche Bank, Santander
- **Benefit**: Enterprise-grade, battle-tested infrastructure

### 5. **AI + Blockchain Synergy**
- **OmniLearn Advantage**: Knowledge graph + Hebbian learning + cryptographic proofs
- **Result**: Smarter compliance, self-improving workflows, audit-ready by default
